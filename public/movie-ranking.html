The best K-dramas to binge over the weekend

Recommendations as of May 2023

Pachinko

Incredible soundtrack, beautiful cinematography, and a raw, powerful Korean immigration and war story that spans across four generations, based on Lee Min Jin's book of the same title.

Number of episodes: 8

The Good Bad Mother

Depicts a complex mother-son relationship that can be relatable to many Asian families. Unexpected plot twists and strong character development.

Number of episodes: 16

Stranger

If you like political and law dramas with a strong plot (and without plot holes), character development, and a stellar cast, this is for you.

Number of seasons: 2
Number of episodes per season: 16
